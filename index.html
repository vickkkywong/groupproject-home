What is Rumors?

a currently circulating story or report of uncertain or doubtful truth.

What is Fake news?

Purposefully crafted, sensational, emotionally charged, misleading or totally fabricated information that mimics the form of mainstream news.

What is Misinformation?

Misinformation is false information spread through media.

What is Disinformation?

False information spread in order to deceive people.

How to address the issue?

    1. Verify information before sharing.
    Verifying information before sharing is an essential practice that helps prevent the spread of false or misleading content.
    2. Report and expose false information promptly.
    Reporting and exposing false information involves identifying and bringing attention to inaccurate or misleading claims or data that have been presented as factual.
    3. Examine the context.
    Consider the context in which the information is presented.
    4. Check the source.
    Investigate the credibility and reputation of the source providing the information.
